Introduction to Hashing

Welcome to the World of Hashing

Defining Hashing: Hashing is a fundamental process in digital security, transforming any data into a fixed-size string of characters. It creates unique identifiers for data, ensuring both confidentiality and integrity.

Applications and Importance: From securing passwords to ensuring data integrity, hashing is ubiquitous in digital technology. It's essential in creating digital signatures, managing data in databases, and securing network communications.

Understanding the Process: This lecture series covers the basics of hashing, its practical applications, and advanced topics like hash collisions and the role of hashing in blockchain and network security.
