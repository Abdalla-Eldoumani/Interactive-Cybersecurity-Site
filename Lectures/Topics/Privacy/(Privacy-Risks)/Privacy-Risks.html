Lesson 2: Privacy Risks

Data Breaches

Data breaches are akin to a bank vault full of valuables with your name on it being cracked open. They can happen to anyone and can lead to crimes like fraud.

Surveillance

Surveillance involves collecting bits of your digital behavior to create a profile. It can be used by companies for advertising or by governments for various reasons.

Identity Theft

Identity theft can ruin your reputation and finances. It's not limited to adults; children are also at risk.

Unwanted Data Collection

Your data, including what you don't see like your IP address, is valuable. Free apps and services often use your data in exchange for their use.

Cookies and Tracking

Cookies are digital breadcrumbs used by websites to remember you, which can be beneficial but also track you across the web.

Phishing and Scams

Phishing is like baiting a hook with a fake email, hoping you'll give away personal info. Scams can be sophisticated and look real.
