Introduction to Privacy

Exploring the Facets of Digital Privacy

Understanding Privacy: Privacy in the digital age is about controlling access to personal space and information. It's akin to safeguarding a secret spot in the digital realm, where personal data resides online.

Privacy Risks: The digital world presents various privacy risks like data breaches, surveillance, identity theft, and unwanted data collection. Understanding these risks is crucial for maintaining personal safety online.

Protecting Your Privacy: Safeguarding digital privacy involves creating strong passwords, using two-factor authentication, being aware of data collection practices, and understanding how cookies and tracking work. It also includes staying vigilant against phishing and scams.

Comprehensive Overview: These lectures will delve into what privacy means in the modern world, the types of risks associated with online activities, and practical strategies for protecting personal information. From the basics of privacy to advanced protective measures, this series aims to empower users to navigate the digital world safely and confidently.
